  1. Position:
    Scriptwriter, Co-director
  2. The Objective:
    To promote KFC’s new Golden Egg Crunch flavour using TV sync technology.

    TV is the number one media being consumed in Malaysia. However, with the rise of multitasking through mobile phones, capturing the attention of the audience has become arduous.
  3. The Idea: 
    The agency, Ensemble Worldwide, approached us with the idea of using TV sync technology to combine storytelling and innovative media platforms to enhance the campaign’s message delivery.

    However, this meant each video had to be written, shot, and edited live.
  4. The Results:
    We successfully delivered 20 creative and cheeky videos on mobile, each pegged to the most popular ads during the campaign period, to air across the social media network within seconds of the live TV commercial trigger.

    Best use of Mobile (Silver)
